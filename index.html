About Me

Hi, I’m Mikaela! I’m a front end web developer from Northern Ontario who now calls Toronto home. My favourite thing about the tech world is the incredible support and encouragement the community provides.

I come from the world of politics where I was so impressed by the impact of emerging technologies that I was inspired to become a positive part of the digital transformation. As a recent graduate of HackerYou’s Web Development Immersive, I'm passionate about deepening my knowledge and applying my skills in a dynamic environment.

When I’m not hunched over my laptop I'm exploring Toronto, planning my next snowboard trip, and staying up to date on current events.

With a little help from coffee.
