About Me

Hi, I’m Mikaela! I’m a front end web developer from Northern Ontario who now calls Toronto home. My favourite thing about the tech world is the incredible support and encouragement the community provides.

I come from the world of politics where I was so impressed by the impact of emerging technologies that I was inspired to become a positive part of the digital transformation. As a recent graduate of HackerYou’s Web Development Immersive, I can’t wait to work on applications that meet the needs of real users.

When I’m not hunched over my laptop I'm exploring Toronto, planning my next snowboard trip, and staying up to date on current events. I have never met a chip I didn't like!

With a little help from coffee.
